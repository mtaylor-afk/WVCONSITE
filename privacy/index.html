Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. Details marked for verification will be confirmed before launch.

This policy explains how WV Construction handles the information you share when you contact us or use the forms on this website.

Who we are

WV Construction is a trading name of ACOR Building & Property Solutions Ltd, registered in England and Wales (company number 09287377). The registered office and data-controller contact details are being verified and will be shown in full before launch.

What we collect

  • Contact enquiries (name, phone, email, postcode and your message)
  • Quote request form details
  • Photo uploads you choose to share
  • Drawing or plan uploads you choose to share
  • Visualiser brief details (project type, style, notes and contact details)

How we use it

We use the information only to respond to your enquiry, discuss and prepare a quote, and carry out any agreed work. We don’t sell your data.

Forms, WhatsApp and email

This site’s forms currently help you send your details to WV Construction by WhatsApp or email — your message is sent using your own app. If a form-submission service is added later, this policy will be updated to describe it.

Visualiser & TailoredQuote

The visualiser image workflow (powered by TailoredQuote) is being connected. Before launch, this section will be updated to document exactly how any uploaded photos and generated concepts are handled. See the Visualiser Terms.

Your rights

You can ask what information we hold about you and request that we correct or delete it. Contact details are on the contact page.
