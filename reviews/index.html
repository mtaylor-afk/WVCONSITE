Why historic

Why we show these historic reviews

These reviews are older public MyBuilder reviews, but they are still useful because they show the types of work and customer themes associated with WV Construction’s trading history: tidy working, punctuality, fair pricing, bathrooms, refurbishment and multi-trade property work. Dates and MyBuilder source links remain visible so you can verify the source.

Honest framing

What these reviews can and cannot tell you

What the reviews help show

  • Historic customer feedback.
  • Types of work customers reviewed.
  • Repeated themes around tidiness and punctuality.
  • Multi-trade refurbishment and bathroom experience.
  • Customers willing to recommend or use again.

What they do not replace

  • A current written quote.
  • Checking current availability.
  • Seeing current project photos.
  • Confirming insurance or accreditations.
  • Drawings, planning, structural or site review where needed.
  • A site visit or proper scope review.
Review questions

Review questions

Are these reviews from MyBuilder?

Yes. The reviews shown on this page are historic public MyBuilder reviews linked to the WV Construction trading profile.

Why are the reviews older?

The visible public MyBuilder reviews are historic. Dates are shown clearly so you can understand the source and age of the feedback.

Can I verify the reviews myself?

Yes. Use the Verify on MyBuilder links to open the public MyBuilder profile and review source.

Do reviews mean my job will cost the same?

No. Every project is different. A current quote depends on scope, drawings, access, materials, property condition and availability.

Do you show all 13 MyBuilder reviews?

This page shows the verified review examples and summaries we could confirm from accessible public source data during build. The MyBuilder profile lists 13 public reviews — use the MyBuilder link to view the full public profile.

What should I send before asking for a quote?

Send your postcode, photos, a short description, any drawings or specification if available, and your preferred timescale.

Confident after reading the reviews?

If you have a project in a CH or L postcode, send WV Construction your postcode, photos and a short description. For extensions or structural work, include drawings if you have them.
