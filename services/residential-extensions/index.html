Extensions

Residential extensions in Wirral & Liverpool

Rear, side and kitchen extensions built from your drawings, with the refurbishment works that go alongside them.

Request a Quote Send Photos by WhatsApp Try the Visualiser

Who it’s for

Homeowners adding space — a bigger kitchen, an open-plan living area or an extra room — who want a builder to carry out the work from their drawings and designer’s pack.

Typical work included

  • Rear, side and wraparound extensions
  • Kitchen extensions and open-plan living
  • Structural openings and internal alterations
  • Foundations, brick and blockwork, roofing
  • First and second fix coordinated with specialist trades
  • Refurbishment and making good around the new space

What we need to quote

  • Your postcode and photos of the area
  • Planning and building-regulations drawings if you have them
  • Structural calculations where relevant
  • A specification or schedule of works if available
  • Your rough timescale
Good to knowExtensions may need planning permission and/or building regulations approval — these are separate matters. Permitted development has limits and conditions. WV Construction can’t confirm whether your project needs approval; that’s checked with your designer and the local authority or building control.

WV Construction has historic MyBuilder review proof (5/5 from 13 reviews) — see reviews. Specialist gas and electrical work is carried out by appropriately qualified trades where required.

Good to know

Questions

Do you provide the drawings?

WV Construction works from your drawings and alongside your designer, engineer and building control provider. We don’t provide architectural or structural-engineering services in-house.

Will I need planning permission?

It depends on the project and property. Planning permission, permitted development and building regulations are separate and may need checking — we can’t confirm this for you.

Can I see the idea first?

Yes — try the visualiser for a concept-style starting point (inspiration only, not a quote or design).
