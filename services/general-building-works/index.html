General building

General building works across CH & L postcodes

Repairs, alterations and small-to-medium building projects for homes and rental properties.

Request a Quote Send Photos by WhatsApp

Who it’s for

Homeowners, landlords and agents who need a capable general builder for a range of works.

Typical work included

  • Repairs and alterations
  • Structural openings (with appropriate input)
  • Brick and blockwork
  • Remedial works
  • Small and medium building projects
  • Making good after other trades

What we need to quote

  • Your postcode and photos
  • A description of the work
  • Any drawings or structural details if relevant
  • Access and timescale
Good to knowStructural openings may need a structural engineer’s calculations and building regulations approval — we’ll tell you where that applies.

WV Construction has historic MyBuilder review proof (5/5 from 13 reviews) — see reviews. Specialist gas and electrical work is carried out by appropriately qualified trades where required.

Questions

What size jobs do you take?

Small and medium projects across CH and L postcodes.

Do you tidy up after?

Yes — tidy working and a clean handover are part of the job.
