Common questions

Do you cover my postcode?

WV Construction focuses on CH and L postcodes only. Send your postcode and details for confirmation.

Can I send photos for a quote?

Yes. Photos help us understand the job and decide whether a site visit or drawings are needed.

Do you work with landlords and letting agents?

Yes — reactive maintenance, end-of-tenancy repairs and void refurbishments, with photo-led triage and access notes.

Is the visualiser a real quote?

No. It is concept/inspiration only and does not confirm cost, planning, building regulations or buildability.

Can you work from drawings?

Yes. We can review existing drawings or a designer’s pack and discuss a build quote. More detail usually helps.
