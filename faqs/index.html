Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Coverage, quotes, the visualiser, drawings, landlords and the company behind WV Construction.

Coverage

Do you cover my postcode?

WV Construction focuses on CH and L postcodes only. Send your postcode and details for confirmation.

Which areas do you serve?

Wirral and Liverpool within CH and L postcodes — for example Wallasey, Birkenhead, Heswall, West Kirby and parts of Liverpool. Send your postcode to confirm.

Quotes

Can I send photos for a quote?

Yes. Photos help WV Construction understand the job and decide whether a site visit or drawings are needed.

Is a quote binding?

A quote isn’t binding until it’s confirmed in writing for an agreed scope of work.

Visualiser

Is the visualiser a real quote?

No. It is concept/inspiration only and does not confirm cost, planning, building regulations or buildability.

Does the visualiser generate an image now?

The image workflow is being connected. For now your photo and brief go straight to a quote conversation.

Extensions, drawings, planning & building regs

Can you work from drawings prepared by someone else?

Yes. WV Construction can review existing drawings or design packs for a build quote discussion. More detail usually helps produce a more realistic scope.

Do I need planning permission?

It depends on the project and property. Planning permission, permitted development and building regulations are separate matters and may need checking. WV Construction can’t confirm this for you.

Do you provide architectural or structural-engineering services?

No. WV Construction works from your drawings and alongside your designer, engineer and building control provider.

Landlords & maintenance

Do you work with landlords and letting agents?

Yes — reactive and planned maintenance, end-of-tenancy repairs and void refurbishments, with photo-led triage and access notes.

Can you support multiple properties?

Yes — repeat support across a CH and L postcode portfolio is welcome.

Trades, reviews & the company

Do you do gas or electrical work?

Specialist gas and electrical work is handled by appropriately qualified trades where required. WV Construction does not claim direct certification.

What is the company behind WV Construction?

WV Construction is a trading name of ACOR Building & Property Solutions Ltd. Final registered-office details should be verified before launch.

Are you fully insured?

Insurance details are being verified before launch. Please ask and WV Construction will confirm.

Are your reviews recent?

The MyBuilder reviews are historic (dates around 2010–2014). They reflect past work under the WV Construction trading profile.

Still have a question?

Send your postcode and ask away — WV Construction is happy to help.
