How the visualiser helps

Upload a photo, choose the type of work you are considering and describe what you want changed. The visualiser creates — or will, once the workflow is connected — a concept-style visual brief that helps WV Construction understand your idea. Any generated concept is for inspiration only and must be checked properly before any design, pricing, planning, structural or building decisions.

Photo tips for the best concept

Works well

  • A clear, daylight photo.
  • The rear elevation, front of the house, garden, garage, side return, room or area you want to change.
  • Enough context to see the whole area.
  • Standing far enough back so walls, roofline, windows, doors, garden/driveway or room layout are visible.
  • For internal refurbishments, the full room from at least one wide angle.

Best avoided

  • Blurry or dark photos.
  • A blocked or partial view.
  • Standing too close to the property or room.
  • Photos with no surrounding context.
  • Images you do not have permission to use.
